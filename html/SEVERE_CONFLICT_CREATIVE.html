Creative Manager Conflict

AntiShare will not run because of this conflict. This conflict is considered critical because it means that you have 2 creative managers, on your server. An example of another creative manager is 'Burning Creative Suite'

AntiShare disables itself because turt2live does not want it to interfere with your other manager(s).

How do I fix this?

You will need to remove the conflicting plugin, or remove AntiShare to stop this conflict.

What plugin is conflicting?

AntiShare detected that -CONFLICT- is on your server.
